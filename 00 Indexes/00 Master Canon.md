00 Master Canon

## Purpose

This note is the master entry point into the *History of Big Ideas* vault. It gathers the full canon in one place and provides the clearest routes into the system: by number, by era, and by domain. It is meant to function as the primary table of contents for the project.

The canon is not simply a list of famous books. It is a structured map of major texts, authors, traditions, and recurring questions. Read from the beginning if you want chronological immersion, by era if you want historical orientation, by domain if you want disciplinary focus, or through the other index and map notes if you want thematic or relational navigation.

## How to Use This Vault

The vault is designed to be entered from more than one direction.

- Use the numbered canon when you want the broad chronological sweep of the tradition.
- Use the era structure when you want to understand how intellectual life changes across time.
- Use the domain structure when you want to focus on a field such as politics, religion, philosophy, literature, science, or psychology.
- Use [[02 Big Ideas Index]] when you want to follow a recurring question such as [[Concept - Justice]], [[Concept - Power]], [[Concept - Truth]], or [[Concept - Meaning]] across many books.
- Use the map notes when you want to trace influence, conceptual movement, or strong book-to-concept connections.
- Use [[03 Reading / Generation Queue]] as the operational note for build status, batching, and workflow progress.

The ideal use of the vault is cumulative. A reader may begin with one book, one idea, one author, or one era, then move through linked notes until a larger historical and conceptual pattern becomes visible.

## Canon by Domain

### Religion & Worldviews
This domain tracks revelation, metaphysics, spiritual discipline, theological order, and ultimate questions of human orientation.

Core route:
[[002 - Hebrew Bible]], [[003 - The Upanishads]], [[004 - The Bhagavad Gita]], [[006 - Tao Te Ching]], [[020 - The New Testament]], [[021 - The Qur'an]], [[022 - The Dhammapada]], [[023 - Confessions]], [[024 - The City of God]], [[029 - Summa Theologiae]]

### Philosophy & Ethics
This domain focuses on truth, reason, virtue, knowledge, moral obligation, and the good life.

Core route:
[[013 - The Republic]], [[015 - Nicomachean Ethics]], [[018 - On the Nature of Things]], [[019 - Meditations]], [[040 - Discourse on Method]], [[041 - Meditations on First Philosophy]], [[048 - A Treatise of Human Nature]], [[056 - Critique of Pure Reason]], [[057 - Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals]], [[099 - A Theory of Justice]]

### Politics & Power
This domain gathers works on rule, sovereignty, law, civic order, empire, liberty, revolution, ideology, and domination.

Core route:

Core route:
[[052 - The Wealth of Nations]], [[053 - The Theory of Moral Sentiments]], [[065 - Capital, Vol. 1]], [[089 - The Road to Serfdom]], [[090 - The General Theory of Employment, Interest and Money]]

This domain examines method, evidence, explanation, causation, scientific revolutions, and the changing authority of knowledge.

Core route:
[[038 - Novum Organum]], [[044 - An Essay Concerning Human Understanding]], [[046 - Principia Mathematica]], [[049 - Enquiry Concerning Human Understanding]], [[063 - On the Origin of Species]], [[096 - The Structure of Scientific Revolutions]], [[097 - Silent Spring]]

Core route:
[[023 - Confessions]], [[078 - The Interpretation of Dreams]], [[079 - The Principles of Psychology]], [[084 - Being and Time]], [[087 - The Myth of Sisyphus]], [[100 - Man's Search for Meaning]]

This domain concerns institutions, social hierarchy, custom, gender, race, punishment, everyday life, and the fabric of collective existence.

This domain gathers the major imaginative works of the canon that carry philosophical, religious, social, and psychological insight through literary form.

Core route:
[[001 - Epic of Gilgamesh]], [[009 - The Iliad]], [[010 - The Odyssey]], [[027 - The Divine Comedy]], [[039 - Don Quixote]], [[071 - The Brothers Karamazov]], [[074 - Anna Karenina]], [[081 - The Trial]], [[083 - Ulysses]], [[094 - 1984]]
